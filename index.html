About Me

Posted on 2023-02-04 Edited on 2023-12-11
I'm Panfeng, a PhD student in the John Hopcroft Center for Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong university, supervised by Biaoshuai Tao. I am mainly interested in social network analyses, and I am currently conducting this study from a theoretical computer science (TCS) perspective. I received my master degree in Computer Technology at Lanzhou University, and received my bachelor degree in Internet of things at Xi'an University of Science and Technology.
